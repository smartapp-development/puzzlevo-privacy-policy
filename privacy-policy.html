Privacy Policy for Puzzlevo

📅 Effective Date: June 12, 2026

Welcome to Puzzlevo! This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application ("Puzzlevo").

📱 Our Apps

This privacy policy applies to the following apps:

Puzzlevo

Note: Puzzlevo is a single app that includes multiple puzzle games (Sudoku, Number Puzzle, Word Search, and Jigsaw Puzzle).

🔒 No Data Collection

Puzzlevo does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users.

We believe in complete privacy. Our app operates without:

  • ❌ User accounts or registration
  • ❌ Login systems
  • ❌ Personal information collection (name, email, phone number, address, etc.)
  • ❌ Location tracking (GPS or IP-based)
  • ❌ Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, Android ID, etc.)
  • ❌ Usage analytics or crash reporting tools
  • ❌ Third-party analytics SDKs
  • ❌ File storage or photo access
  • ❌ Contact list or calendar access
  • ❌ Microphone or camera access

📊 Game Data

All game progress, moves, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

📢 Advertising

Puzzlevo uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain anonymous data to serve relevant ads. This is handled entirely by Google and subject to Google's privacy policy.

What Google AdMob may collect:

  • Anonymous device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address - city/country level only)
  • Advertising ID (resettable by user)

You can learn more about Google's data collection here: Google Privacy Policy

To opt out of personalized advertising, visit: Google Ad Settings

🔧 Permissions Used

Puzzlevo requires no special permissions. The app works fully offline without needing internet access (ads require internet).

👶 Children's Privacy

Puzzlevo does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no data whatsoever, no special parental controls or consent are required. The app is safe for all ages.

🌐 Third-Party Services

Puzzlevo integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services - For app distribution and updates
  • Google AdMob - For displaying advertisements

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Data Collection

🛡️ Data Security

Because Puzzlevo collects no personal or sensitive data, there is no risk of data breach, unauthorized access, or data misuse through our app. Your privacy is completely protected by design.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
